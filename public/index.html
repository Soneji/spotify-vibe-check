Some info on the app:

Spotify vibe checker was created with the official Spotify API and generates statistics about the nature of your playlists. The characteristics generated are shown below

  • Average Playlist Energy represents a perceptual measure of intensity and activity. Typically, energetic tracks feel fast, loud, and noisy. For example, death metal has high energy, while a Bach prelude scores low on the scale. Perceptual features contributing to this attribute include dynamic range, perceived loudness, timbre, onset rate, and general entropy.
  • Average Playlist Danceability describes how suitable a track is for dancing based on a combination of musical elements including tempo, rhythm stability, beat strength, and overall regularity.
  • Average Playlist Vibes – Tracks with high vibes sound more positive (e.g. happy, cheerful, euphoric), while tracks with low vibes sound more negative (e.g. sad, depressed,angry).
